School History

Fr. Scheffer Boys Boarding (also known as Father Scheffer Asumbi Boys Boarding) is a public primary school located in Homa Bay County, Kenya, specifically within the Rangwe Constituency . It is closely associated with the Catholic mission at Asumbi.

Founder and Context: The school is named after Fr. Philip Scheffer, a dedicated Catholic missionary priest who worked in South Kavirondo (present-day Homa Bay and Migori Counties) [04.6]. He was known for his dedication to evangelization, often traveling long distances between Asumbi, Tonga, and Karungu [04.6].
